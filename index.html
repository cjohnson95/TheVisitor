The Visitor

Lisa was alone one night, house-sitting for a friend. While snuggling in for a movie, a knock came at the door.

Confused, Lisa cautiously rose from the couch, hoping to sneak a peak of the unexpected visitor.

To Lila's surprise, it was her friend whom she was house-sitting for. Lila relaxed and opened the door.

"What are you doing back so early, I didn't expect you for two more days." Lila's friend said nothing, but only wore an eerie smile on her face as she walked in.

As the friend retreated further into the house, Lila followed her puzzled at her odd behavior. "Hello! Did you hear me? What's up?" Lila's friend still didn't respond, only retreated further into the house.

Just as Lila was nearing her friend's bedroom, her phone rang. The caller ID read her friend's name. "Odd", Lila thought to herself. "Are you messing with me?" Lila called out to her friend. No response. The phone rang again. This time, Lila answered. "What game are you playing?" Lila asked confused. "Lila! Get out now! I got a notification of a visitor and looked at my camera footage. I don't know who that is, but it isn't me. Leave.Now."
